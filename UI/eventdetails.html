About This Event

Join us for an unforgettable night of music, lights, and celebration at the Summer Night Festival 2023. Experience performances from world-class artists across three stages in the heart of Central Park.

This year's lineup features chart-topping headliners and emerging talents in electronic, pop, and indie genres. Immerse yourself in stunning light installations, interactive art displays, and gourmet food and drink options.
