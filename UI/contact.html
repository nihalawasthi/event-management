Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our support number.

Can I change or cancel my event registration?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your registration up to 72 hours before the event. Please contact our support team for assistance.

How do I become an event organizer?

To become an organizer, create an account and select "Organizer" as your account type. You can then access the event creation tools.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. Download it from the App Store or Google Play for on-the-go access.
